Bio

Trevor is a sophomore English major with a creative writing emphasis and a Marin native. He enjoys writing both prose and poetry, but has focused this semester on developing a stronger poetic direction. He is influenced by many genres of literature, some of his favorite writers include E. E. Cummings, Emil Cioran. Joe Abercrombie, and Leslie Marmon Silko. He also has academic interests in social action, brutalist architecture, politics, and philosophy.
